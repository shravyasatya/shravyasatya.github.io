-BOOKS-

My favorite book is "The Summer I Turned Pretty" by Jenny Han. It's a three book series and I love all three of the books. It's young adult fiction and romance and is very very interesting. I usually don't spend a lot of my time reading books, but when I was reading The Summer I Turned Pretty series, reading was all I was doing. I also usually don't get emotional to books. but this series actually made me cry. The book series also has a TV series on Prime that I haven't watched yet. I'm definitely looking forward to watching that and it will go in my TV show favorites list.
